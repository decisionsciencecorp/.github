## What we do

**Decision Science Corp** operates where human analysis becomes uneconomic — large messy datasets, compressed timelines, and uncertainty that has to be **quantified**, not guessed.

In practice that means:

- **Applied data science** — structure discovery, robustness checks, and decision-ready artifacts (not dashboard theater)
- **Product engineering** — web apps, APIs, integrations, and operator tooling shipped on our multihost stack
- **Agent-native systems** — SMCP plugins, SDKs, and documentation for teams automating real workflows

We work in short cycles with explicit assumptions and confidence bounds. You own your data and models.
